Lara Dominguez

explores themes of loneliness, nostalgia, uncertainty and light. Her self-processed film photos aim to capture the internal reflection of letting go of an assumed identity to find quiet and unsophisticated simplicity. Soft focus, dust and scratches from film are all part of the process and documentation of her identity and surroundings. Lara enjoys finding poetry and narrative in small moments of everyday life, simple gestures, intimacy and detachment.
